Artiq Metal Coatings, based in Dubai, is a premier service provider specializing in exquisite metal finishes. Our expertise encompasses the artistry of antique brass, the opulence of gold plating, the functionality of copper plating, and bespoke DIY coatings. With a commitment to quality and aesthetics, Artiq transforms metal surfaces, ensuring durability and captivating visual appeal. Discover a fusion of craftsmanship and innovation with Artiq Metal Coatings, where every finish tells a story of precision and elegance
